# Integration with Human Meaning Network (HMN)

AIC-Recovery is tightly integrated with HMN to ensure that even in worst-case scenarios, the network can recover while preserving human meaning and ethical purpose.

**Integration Points**:
- Recovery triggers HMN journaling to record meaningful events.
- Ethical rollback checks against HMN’s meaning metrics.
- Self-healing maintains the overall coherence of the Human Meaning Network.

This integration ensures that recovery is not just technical, but also preserves the deeper purpose of HMN.
